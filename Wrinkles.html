Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)

banana

1/2 cup Greek yogurt

1 tablespoon chia seeds

1/2 cup almond milk

Instructions

1. Blend all the ingredients until smooth

2. Enjoy this antioxidant-rich smoothie that's packed with vitamins, fiber, and healthy fats.

X

Salmon Salad

Ingredients

Grilled or baked salmon fillet

Mixed greens (spinach, kale, arugula)

Sliced avocado,Cherry tomatoes

Olive oil and lemon juice dressing

Instructions

1. Arrange the greens, avocado, tomatoes, and salmon on a plate

2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice for a dose of omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins

Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients

Cooked quinoa

Assorted colorful vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)

Tofu or lean chicken breast

Low-sodium soy sauce or teriyaki sauce

Instructions

1. Stir-fry the vegetables and protein source with a little sauce

2. Serve over quinoa for a meal rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins

Spinach and Tomato Omelette

Ingredients

Eggs

Handful of spinach

Sliced tomatoes

Feta cheese (optional)

Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

1. Whisk eggs and cook them with spinach and sliced tomatoes

2. Add feta cheese if desired and cook until set

3. Eggs provide protein, while spinach and tomatoes contribute vitamins and antioxidants

Avocado Toast with Eggs

Ingredients

Whole-grain toast

Ripe avocado, mashed

Poached or fried egg

Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

1. Spread mashed avocado on the toast

2. Top with a poached or fried egg and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes

3. Avocado offers healthy fats and vitamins, while eggs provide protein

Brightening Turmeric and Lemon Face Mask

Ingredients

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1 tablespoon yogurt

Few drops of lemon juice

Instructions

1. Mix the turmeric, yogurt, and lemon juice to form a paste

2. Apply the mask to your face and leave it on for 10-15 minutes

3. Rinse off with cool water
