Honey Lemon Ginger Tea

Ingredients

1 tablespoon of honey

1 cup of water

Juice of half a lemon

1 teaspoon of freshly grated ginger (or a slice of ginger)

Instructions

1. Boil the water and let it cool slightly

2. Add the honey, lemon juice, and grated ginger to the water.

3. Stir well until the honey is dissolved.

4. Sip on the warm honey lemon ginger tea slowly to soothe your sore throat.

Fenugreek Tea

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of fenugreek seeds

1 cup of water

1 teaspoon of honey (optional)

Instructions

1. Boil the water and add the fenugreek seeds.

2. Let the seeds simmer for about 5-10 minutes.

3. Strain the tea into a cup.

4. Add honey if desired for sweetness and added soothing properties.

5. Sip on the warm fenugreek tea slowly to relieve your sore throat.

Chamomile Peppermint Tea

Ingredients

1 chamomile tea bag

1 peppermint tea bag

1 cup of hot water

1 tablespoon of honey (optional)

Instructions

1. Place the chamomile and peppermint tea bags in a cup & Pour hot water over the tea

2. Let the tea steep for about 5-7 minutes.

4. Remove the tea bags and add honey if desired

Garlic Honey Remedy

Ingredients

2-3 cloves of garlic

1/4 cup of honey

Instructions

1. Peel and crush the garlic cloves.

2. Place the crushed garlic in a clean, dry glass jar.

3. Pour honey over the garlic until it's fully covered.

4. Seal the jar and let it sit for a day or two to allow the flavors to infuse.

5. Take a teaspoon of the garlic-infused honey as needed

Saltwater Gargle

Ingredients

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup warm water

Instructions

1. Mix 1/2 teaspoon of salt in a glass of warm water.

2. Gargle with the mixture for 15-30 seconds.

3. Spit out the water, but do not swallow it.

4. Repeat several times a day.

Green Tea

Ingredients

1 cup shredded chicken

1 cup mixed vegetables

1 clove garlic,minced

Salt and pepper,turmeric

Instructions

1. Saute the minced garlic in a little oil until fragrant.

2. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they start to soften.

3. Add the shredded chicken and turmeric to the pot.

4. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes, until the flavors meld together.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
