Salt Water Gargle

Ingredients

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup warm water

Instructions

1. Dissolve the salt in warm water

2. Rinse your mouth with the saltwater solution for about 30 seconds

3. Spit out the solution

4. Repeat 2-3 times a day

Turmeric Mask

Ingredients

1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder

Water

Instructions

1. Mix turmeric powder with a small amount of water to create a paste.

2. Apply the paste to your gums

3. Leave it on for a few minutes

4. Gently rinse your mouth with water

5. Use this remedy 2-3 times a week

Oil Pulling

Ingredients

1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions

1.Take a tablespoon of coconut oil and swish it in your mouth for about 15 minutes

2.Spit out the oil

3.Rinse your mouth with warm water

4.Perform oil pulling once daily in the morning before brushing your teeth
